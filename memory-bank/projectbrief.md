# Project Brief - Instagram Cannabis Brand Strategy

## Project Overview
Developing and executing a comprehensive Instagram strategy for Smoothie Bar, a cannabis vape brand specializing in dual-tank products that allow users to experience two strains separately or together.

## Core Mission
Build an authentic LA cannabis brand presence through educational content, lifestyle documentation, and community engagement that positions Smoothie Bar as the curator of authentic LA cannabis culture.

## Target Audience
Cannabis-positive LA residents aged 21-35, including emerging creatives, industry professionals, and lifestyle enthusiasts who value authenticity over mainstream cannabis marketing.

## Unique Value Proposition
The only cannabis brand authentically documenting real LA cannabis culture through:
- Individual lifestyle stories
- Street-level perspectives  
- Educational content that builds trust through expertise rather than hype
- Dual-tank technology that respects individual strain character while exploring combinations

## Key Product Focus
**Smoothie Bar: Blend Collection** - Dual-tank ceramic-coil vape devices featuring carefully curated strain combinations that tell cultural and flavor stories.

## Strategic Positioning
- Authentic voice of LA cannabis culture
- Curator/tastemaker rather than pushy advertiser
- Educational authority building trust through expertise
- Focus on craftsmanship and curation process
- Professional tone with strategic authenticity

## Success Metrics
- Quality engagement over follower count
- Brand association with authentic LA culture
- Community trust and participation
- Educational content authority
- Product inquiry and sales attribution
