# Smoothie Bar Product Description

## Consumer-Facing Description

The Smoothie Bar: Blend 2.0 is a dual-tank, postless ceramic-coil vaporizer that delivers a new way to explore two premium cannabis concentrates: savor each strain's unique character individually or BLEND to discover a carefully curated flavor synergy. Unlike traditional single-strain vapes, the dual-tank design highlights individual live resin profiles, while delivering a powerful yet satisfying and smooth experience. Each device is designed with a tactile switch that controls the BLEND function and an intuitive screen featuring a battery power percent level and arrows that indicate the selected strain. With the Blend 2,0, Smoothie Bar combines technological innovation with deep industry experience to showcase the absolute best strain combinations. With each BLEND, Smoothie Bar draws from authentic LA cannabis culture layered with the rich history and diversity of global cannabis traditions."
