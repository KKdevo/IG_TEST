"Will it Blend?" 

It's the question behind every Smoothie Bar: Blend product, and we're bringing you along for the journey that follows every strain pairing we create. 

Not every pairing works. Some strains clash, others complement, and a few create something entirely new. In these posts, we'll dive into why we believe certain strains belong together and go behind-the-scenes of the Smoothie Bar strain selection process.

First, an important note on technology - our dual-tank, ceramic-coil device is crucial to testing each strain solo before experiencing them together. Our selection process considers all these elements: Do the terpenes complement or contrast in interesting ways? Do their origins tell a compelling story together? Most importantly, do they maintain their individual character while creating something new?
