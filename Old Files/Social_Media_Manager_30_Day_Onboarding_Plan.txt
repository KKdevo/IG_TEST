# SOCIAL MEDIA MANAGER 30-DAY ONBOARDING PLAN
## Complete Implementation Guide for Instagram Cannabis Brand Strategy

### DOCUMENT PURPOSE
This plan provides day-by-day action items for a Social Media Manager to coordinate team implementation of the complete Instagram cannabis brand strategy within 30 days. You will be directing a media team and a posting team member to execute all 5 content pillars while building sustainable systems.

---

## OVERVIEW: YOUR ROLE & RESPONSIBILITIES

### PRIMARY RESPONSIBILITIES
- **Strategy Coordination**: Direct team execution of 5-pillar content strategy
- **Team Management**: Coordinate media team (content creators) and posting team member
- **Production Oversight**: Ensure quality content creation across all pillars
- **Performance Management**: Track metrics and optimize strategy
- **Legal Compliance**: Ensure all content meets cannabis industry regulations
- **Community Building**: Direct authentic LA cannabis culture representation

### TEAM STRUCTURE YOU'LL MANAGE
- **Media Team**: Content creators, videographers, photographers
- **Posting Team Member**: Handles daily posting and initial community management
- **Legal/Compliance**: Review content for regulatory compliance
- **You**: Strategic oversight, coordination, performance optimization

### SUCCESS METRICS (30-Day Goals)
- All 5 content pillars actively producing content
- 2-3 posts per week consistently scheduled
- Community engagement systems operational
- Performance tracking systems established
- Team workflows optimized and sustainable
