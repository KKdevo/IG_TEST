## TROUBLESHOOTING GUIDE

### COMMON IMPLEMENTATION CHALLENGES

**Challenge**: Team members unclear on brand voice
**Solution**: Conduct additional brand voice training sessions, create more examples, practice writing exercises

**Challenge**: Content production bottlenecks
**Solution**: Review workflow efficiency, redistribute responsibilities, add quality control checkpoints

**Challenge**: Low community engagement
**Solution**: Analyze content performance, adjust posting times, improve community management, increase authentic interactions

**Challenge**: Legal compliance concerns
**Solution**: Review all content against compliance checklist, consult legal team, update training and procedures

**Challenge**: Performance tracking difficulties
**Solution**: Simplify tracking systems, focus on key metrics, provide additional analytics training

### ESCALATION PROCEDURES
- **Immediate Issues**: Contact team lead within 2 hours
- **Legal/Compliance Issues**: Contact legal team immediately
- **Crisis Situations**: Activate crisis management protocol
- **Performance Issues**: Schedule team review meeting within 24 hours
