Flexible Content Calendar Framework

Document Purpose
This framework provides a dynamic, opportunity-based approach to content planning that maintains strategic balance while allowing for creative freedom and real-time adaptation.

FRAMEWORK OVERVIEW

Core Philosophy
- Content driven by opportunity, not rigid schedule
- Maintain pillar balance over time, not daily requirements
- React to engagement and community response
- Leverage cultural moments and business needs
- Track performance to guide decisions

FLEXIBLE CONTENT PLANNING SYSTEM

Content Pillar Balance Targets

Weekly Targets (Flexible)
- Street Interviews/Sampling: 30-40% of content
- Educational Content: 20-30% of content
- Community Engagement: 20-25% of content
- Lifestyle Interviews: 10-15% of content (when active)
- Flavor Insights: 10-15% of content

Monthly Review and Adjustment
- Analyze which pillars performed best
- Adjust following month's focus based on performance
- Identify content gaps and opportunities
- Plan special campaigns and collaborations

Opportunity-Based Content Planning

High-Engagement Opportunities
- Trending topics in cannabis community
- Viral moments or memes to participate in
- Breaking news in cannabis industry
- Community events and gatherings

Real-Time Pivot Guidelines
- If street interview goes viral, create follow-up content
- If educational post gets high engagement, dive deeper
- If community asks questions, create educational response
- If lifestyle subject gains following, feature them again

Content Balance Monitoring

Weekly Content Audit Questions
- Are we maintaining pillar balance over time?
- Which content types are getting best engagement?
- Are we missing any cultural or business opportunities?
- Is our content feeling fresh and unpredictable?
- Are we responding to community interests?

Monthly Strategic Review
- Overall content performance analysis
- Audience growth and engagement trends
- Content pillar effectiveness assessment
- Upcoming opportunity identification
- Strategy adjustments for following month

IMPLEMENTATION GUIDELINES

Setting Up the Flexible System

Calendar Setup
- Create master calendar with all holidays and cultural moments
- Add internal business deadlines and milestones
- Set up monthly planning sessions
- Establish weekly content review meetings

Performance Tracking Setup
- Set up analytics tracking tools
- Create performance tracking templates
- Establish reporting schedules
- Define success metrics and KPIs

Team Workflow Integration
- Train team on flexible planning approach
- Establish communication protocols for pivots
- Create approval processes for opportunity content
- Set up real-time collaboration tools

Monthly Planning Process

Month-End Review (Last week of month)
- Analyze previous month's performance
- Identify upcoming opportunities and deadlines
- Set content pillar targets for following month
- Plan major campaigns and collaborations

Month-Start Planning (First week of month)
- Finalize monthly content themes
- Identify key cultural moments to leverage
- Set up tracking and monitoring systems
- Brief team on monthly priorities

Mid-Month Check-in (Third week of month)
- Review performance against targets
- Identify pivot opportunities
- Adjust remaining month's content focus
- Plan for following month's opportunities

Real-Time Adaptation Guidelines

When to Pivot Content Strategy
- Viral moment or trending topic emerges
- Unexpected high engagement on specific content type
- Community requests or questions arise
- Breaking news or industry developments
- Seasonal or cultural moments present opportunities

How to Maintain Strategic Balance
- Track content pillar distribution over time
- Ensure educational content maintains regular presence
- Balance entertainment with information
- Maintain authentic LA culture representation
- Keep community engagement consistent

Quality Control in Flexible System
- Maintain brand voice consistency across all content
- Ensure legal compliance regardless of content type
- Keep production quality standards high
- Verify all content aligns with brand values
- Monitor community response and adjust accordingly

This flexible framework allows for creative freedom while ensuring strategic objectives are met and no important opportunities are missed. The system adapts to performance data and community response while maintaining the authentic, spontaneous feel that makes LA cannabis culture unique.
