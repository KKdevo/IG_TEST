Integrated Loyalty System: Giveaways, Points Program & Brand Engagement Framework

Project Context
Building on the established Instagram cannabis brand strategy, this document develops a comprehensive customer loyalty system that combines traditional giveaways with a QR code-based points program. This integrated approach creates multiple touchpoints for community engagement, rewards customer loyalty, and drives repeat purchases while maintaining strong brand visibility.

Core Concept Recap
Vision: Create a seamless loyalty ecosystem where customers earn points through QR code scans on products, engage with social media content, participate in giveaways, and redeem rewards through a tiered system that encourages long-term brand loyalty.

Format: Integrated system combining QR code points earning, tiered merchandise rewards, traditional giveaways, company updates, interactive polls, and community celebrations.

Strategic Goal: Build engaged community, drive repeat purchases, gather market insights, increase customer lifetime value, and create brand ambassadors through a comprehensive loyalty program that rewards both purchases and engagement.
